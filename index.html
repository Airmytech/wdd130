Delta State

Delta State is one of Nigeria's 36 states, located in the South-South region of the country. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, diverse ethnic groups, and abundant natural resources, particularly oil and gas. The state is also home to beautiful rivers, wetlands, and vibrant communities, making it an important center for commerce, agriculture, and tourism.
